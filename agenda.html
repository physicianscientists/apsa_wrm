Instructions for student speaker and presenters

We will notify the selected presenters at the latest by Sept 3rd.


Student SpeakersPlease submit the ppts 24 hours beforehand in ppt form. We will have them loaded in order of presentations. Please submit them in the format LAST NAME_FIRST NAME_PRESENTATION TITLE to south@physicianscientists.org. Please bring a backup -- your presentation on a USB just in case. Faculty judges will score your presentations and select three best presentations that will be announced during our last session.

Poster PresentersPosters should be no larger than 4’x4’. Push pins will be provided for use in top right of your respective poster board. Please see registration table at check in for location of your respective poster. Faculty and peer judges will score your poster presentations and select three best presentations that will be announced during our last session.
