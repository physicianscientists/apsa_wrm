APSA is coming to Houston!

Register now for the Southern Regional Meeting of the American Physician Scientists Association!

Register Now!

Join us in the world's largest medical center!

The Baylor College of Medicine MSTP will host the 2018 APSA Southern Regional Meeting in Houston, TX on Saturday, September 29, 2018. Registration is open to current and prospective medical students and dual-degree candidates, as well as residents, fellows, and faculty. The meeting will include several keynote scientific and career development talks, student posters and oral presentations from many different institutions and departments, and breakout sessions on topics relevant to multiple stages of training.

We look forward to seeing you there!
